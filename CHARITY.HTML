Welcome to children hope foundation

save the children save the world

Our Foundation is a private not-for-profit and non-government organization based and operating in Dodoma the capital city of Tanzania in East Africa.
We aim at reaching marginalized children and youths such as orphans, school drop outs and members of very poor families.
Today we bring together over 100 children from the outskirt villages of Dodoma to provide them with the basics of life that include; education, shelter, medication, food, clothing, parental love and care.
